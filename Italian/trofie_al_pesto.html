trofie al pesto

  • 1 recipe handmade Trofie pasta
  • 2 cups packed fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Red pepper flakes, to taste
  • Â¼ cup freshly grated parmesan cheese

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add in the handmade Trofie pasta and cook to al dente, about 4-5 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, pulse the basil, garlic, pine nuts, and parmesan cheese in a food processor until coarsely chopped.
  4. Add in the olive oil and process until fully incorporated and smooth.
  5. Taste then season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.Once cooked, drain the pasta and place it in a large bowl.
  6. Pour the pesto over the cooked pasta and stir to fully combine. Serve hot or cold.
