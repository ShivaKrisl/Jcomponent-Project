cloud eggs

  • 4 large eggs separated
  • 1/4 tbspn Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tbspn salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbspn fresh basil
  • 1 tbspn oil
  • Energy 70j
  • Total Fat 5.0g
  • Cholestero 185mg
  • Sodium 70mg
  • Total Carbohydrate 0.0g
  • Vitamin D 1mcg
  • vitamin A 0%
  1. Preheat over to 450 degrees.
  2. Separate eggs, place whites in a large bowl and yolks in 4 separate small bowls.
  3. Beat egg whites with Italian seasoning, salt and pepper unitl still peaks form.
  4. In an oven safe pan coated with cooking spray, drop egg white mixture in 4 mounds. With the back of a spoon, create a small well in the center of each mound.
  5. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake until light brown, about 5 minutes. Pull from oven and then gently slip egg yolk into each of the mounds.
  6. Bake until yolks are set abpit 3 - 5 minutes. Sprinkle with basil and tomoatoes. Serve with a glass of Arrossire di Barbera!
