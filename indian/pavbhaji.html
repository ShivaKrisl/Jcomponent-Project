Pav Bhaji

  • Pavs 8
  • Potatoes boiled and mashed 2 cups
  • Cauliflower grated 3 tablespoons
  • Capsicum finely chopped 1/4 cup
  • Oil 3 tablespoons
  • Onions chopped 1 cup
  • Ginger-garlic paste 1 1/2 teaspoons
  • Tomato puree fresh 1 cup
  • Readymade tomato puree 1/4 cup
  • Salt to taste
  • Pav bhaji masala 1 1/2 tablespoons
  • Red chilli powder 1/2 teaspoon
  • Lemon juice 2 teaspoons
  • Butter 2 tablespoons
  • A few sprigs fresh coriander leaves
  • A lemon slice
  • Calories 100g
  • Total Fat 5.0g
  • Cholesterol 250mg
  • Sodium 450.0mg
  • Total Carbohydrate 11.0g
  • Vitamin C 10%
  • Vitamin A 6%
  1. Heat oil in a non-stick pan, add 他 cup chopped onions and saute. When they turn golden add ginger-garlic paste and saute.
  2. Add cauliflower and saute. Add mashed potatoes and mix. Add fresh tomato puree and ready made tomato puree and mix well. Add 他 cup water and mix and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add green capsicum and mix well. Cover and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add red chilli powder and lemon juice and mix. Add butter and mix
  5. Chop coriander leaves and mix.
  6. In another pan slightly toast pavs with butter.
  7. Transfer into a serving bowl, garnish with remaining onion and a lemon slice and serve hot with pavs.
