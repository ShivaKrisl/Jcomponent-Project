Jajangmyeon

  • 300gm pork
  • 3 potatoes
  • 2 large onions
  • 3 handful of cabbage
  • 300gm chungjang
  • oil
  • 1 green onion
  • 500ml water
  • 2-3 tablespoons of slurry
  • 3-4 tablespoons of sugar
  • Noodles
  • Cucumber for topping
  • Calories 610
  • Total Fat 23.0g
  • Cholesterol 30mg
  • Sodium 1060mg
  • Total Carbohydrate 91.0g
  • Vitamin C 2%
  • Calcium 4%
  1. Dice all the veggie ingredients.
  2. Fry the chungjang with oil on low heat until glossy for 10minutes and keep the excess oil and glossy fried chungjang in seperate bowls.
  3. Take another pan and coat it with oil, fry the diced green onion.
  4. When fragrant, add pork, diced veggies, starting from the hardest to softest, chungjang and sugar and keep frying.
  5. Add and boil water, add slurry to thicken the sauce.
  6. Boil the noodles and rinse in cold water to avoid overcooking of noodles.
  7. Serve the noodles with jajang sauce and garnish with cucumber.
