Quesadillas

  • 1 packet tortillas
  • 2 cups cheddar cheese
  • 100 gm chicken
  • 1 bowl hung yogurt
  • 2 tsp coriander
  • 2 tsp spring onion
  • 2 green chillies
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • Sour cream
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • A pinch of chilli powder
  • A pinch of onion salt
  • A pinch of oregano
  • Calories 940
  • Total Fat 53g
  • Cholesterol 38mg
  • Sodium 1780mg
  • Total Carbohydrate 55g
  • Vitamin C 0%
  • Vitamin A 0%
  1. Place all ingredients together.
  2. Boil chicken and shred. Sprinkle the taco powder over the chicken.
  3. Whisk the coriander, spring onion and chillies into the hung yogurt. Season with salt.
  4. Open a flour tortilla and layer with the yogurt mix. Add a little cheeses and top with chicken or mushrooms cooked in garlic and sprinkled with taco powder.
  5. Sprinkle more cheese.Heat very little oil and cook till light brown. Flip.
  6. Take the tortilla out and cut into triangles. Serve with sour cream, salsa, hot sauce or guacamole.
