egg burrito

  • 12 bacon strips, chopped
  • 12 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 10 flour tortillas (8 inches), warmed
  • 1-1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Calories 590
  • Total Fat 28.00g
  • Cholesterol 380mg
  • Sodium 1000mg
  • Total Carbohydrate 55g
  • Vitamin C 0%
  • Vitamin A 0%
  1. In a large cast-iron or other heavy skillet, cook bacon until crisp; drain on paper towels.
  2. Remove all but 1-2 tablespoons drippings from pan.
  3. Whisk together eggs, salt and pepper.
  4. Heat skillet over medium heat; pour in egg mixture.
  5. Cook and stir until eggs are thickened and no liquid egg remains; remove from heat.
  6. Spoon about 1/4 cup egg mixture onto center of each tortilla; sprinkle with cheese, bacon and green onions. Roll into burritos.
