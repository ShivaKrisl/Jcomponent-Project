Rancheros

  • 5 tomatillos, husked and halved
  • 2 tbsp coarsely onion
  • 1 to 2 serrano peppers
  • 3 garlic cloves,
  • 1 spoon chicken bouillon granules
  • 15 ounce seasoned recipe black beans
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded Manchego cheese
  • 8 tostada shells, warmed
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Chopped tomato, sliced
  • avocado and minced fresh cilantro, optional
  • Calories 322
  • Total Fat 21.5g
  • Cholesterol 118.5mg
  • Sodium 780.5mg
  • Total Carbohydrate 19.5g
  • Vitamin C 11.5%
  • Vitamin A 26%
  1. Place the tomatillos, onion, pepper, garlic and bouillon in a food processor.
  2. Cover and process until finely chopped; set aside. In a small saucepan, mash beans.
  3. Cook on low until heated through, stirring occasionally.
  4. Meanwhile, break eggs in batches into a large nonstick skillet coated with cooking spray.
  5. Cover and cook over low heat for 5-7 minutes or until eggs are set. Sprinkle with cheese.
  6. To serve, spread beans over tostada shells; top with eggs, tomatillo sauce and sour cream. Garnish with tomato, avocado and cilantro if desired.
